Ingredients for Pork Adobo

  • 2 lbs pork belly or shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Ingredients for Fried Chicken

  • 4 chicken pieces (legs, thighs, or breasts)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Oil for frying

Ingredients for Ice Cream - Vanilla Flavor

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Ingredients for Fries

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt
  • Optional: pepper, paprika, garlic powder, or other seasonings
